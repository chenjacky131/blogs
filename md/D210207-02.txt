Linux常用命令
----------

by jacky at 2021/02/07

1.查找文件位置
````
find / -name filename
````
2.重启mysql
````
service mysqld restart
````
3.查看mysql状态
````
service mysqld status
````

4.关闭防火墙
````
 systemctl stop firewalld
````

5.删除
````
rm -rf filename -r递归 -f强制删除
````

6.保存
````
wq

````

7.不保存退出
````
q!
````
