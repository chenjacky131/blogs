css3的border-image属性
----------

by jacky at 2018/10/17

&nbsp;

border-image:border-image-source border-image-slice/ border-image-width/ border-image-outlet stretch(默认)|repeat|round;

border-image-slice边框图片裁切，border-iamge-width根据这个值来缩放边框图片，border-image-outlet边框图片偏离边框的位置，不能为负值。
